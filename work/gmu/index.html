GMU Classwork

GMU College of Science

The George Mason University brand underwent drastic changes in the summer of 2024. As a Graphics Assistant for Mason's College of Science (COS), I had to start using all-new colors, fonts, graphic elements and logos in my work. This proved to be a challenge at first since the new guidelines were stricter than before. However, with the help of my team I was able to develop a visual identity for COS social media posts that was conformative yet innovative.
